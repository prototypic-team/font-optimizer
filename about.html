Font Optimizer

What it does

Font Optimizer helps you shrink font files for the web. You open a font from your computer, browse its glyphs, turn off the ones you do not need, and export a subset as WOFF and WOFF2 in a single zip. Smaller fonts mean less data for visitors to download and faster page loads.

How it works

Drop a font onto the page or pick files with the keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+U on Windows and Linux, ⌘U on Mac). The app shows glyphs in groups so you can disable whole ranges or individual characters. When you are ready, export builds new font files that include only the glyphs you left enabled. You can also copy glyph selections between fonts when you are working with a family.

Privacy

Processing happens in your browser. Font Optimizer does not upload your files to a server; your fonts stay on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Supported formats

You can open common web and desktop formats (including WOFF2, WOFF, TTF, OTF, and some collection font files). Export is WOFF and WOFF2 for use with @font-face on the web.

Licenses

Subsetting does not change a font’s license. You still need permission to use and embed the font on your site according to the foundry or author’s terms.
